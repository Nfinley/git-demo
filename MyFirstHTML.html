How to Make a Sandwhich:

  1. Gather all of our ingredients
  2. Then take two slices of bread and put all your condiments on it
  3. Then add your meat of choice or veggies
  4. Place the top slice of bread on your sandwich
  5. Cut diagonally because that is the only way to do it
